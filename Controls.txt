Joysticks - make the drive go
Right Bumber - Coral intake - Move elevator to preset position rotate the angler to preset angle and start take
Left Bumber - Algae intake - Move elevator to preset position rotate the angler to preset angle and start take
Right Trigger - Coral outtake - spin motor to shoot out the coral (checkmark)
Left Trigger - Algae outtake - spin motor to shoot out algae (checkmark)
Triangle Button - Depending on the mode it can either Set height to get algae or set height to score coral (half check mark)
Circle Button - Change the mode Algae to Coral and Coral to Algae (half checkmark)
